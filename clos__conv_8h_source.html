70/// Performs *typed closure conversion*.
71/// This is based on the [Simply Typed Closure Conversion](https://dl.acm.org/doi/abs/10.1145/237721.237791).
72/// Closures are represented using dependent pairs `[env_type:*, cn[env_type, Args..], env_type]`.
73/// In general only *continuations* are converted (i.e Lam%s that have type `cn[...]`).
74/// Different kind of Lam%s may be rewritten differently:
75/// - *returning continuations* ("functions"), *join-points* and *branches* are fully closure converted.
76/// - *return continuations* are not closure converted.
77/// - *first-class continuations* get a "dummy" closure, they still have free variables.
78///
79/// This pass relies on ClosConvPrep to introduce annotations for these cases.
80///

82/// `ax : (B : *, int -> B) -> (int -> B)` won't be converted, possible arguments may.

84/// problems with polymorphic functions.
