DefSet & thorin::clos::FreeDefAna::run ( Lam *  lam)

FreeDefAna::run will compute free defs (FD) that appear in lams body.

Nominal Defs are only considered free if they are annotated with Clos::freeBB or Clos::fstclassBB. Otherwise, we add a nom's free defs in order to build a closure for it. Structural Defs containing nominals are broken up if necessary.
