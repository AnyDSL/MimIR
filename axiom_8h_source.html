33 /// The layout is as follows:
34 /// ```
35 /// |---7--||---6--||---5--||---4--||---3--||---2--||---1--||---0--|
36 /// 7654321076543210765432107654321076543210765432107654321076543210
37 /// Char67Char66Char65Char64Char63Char62Char61Char60|---reserved---|
38 /// ```
39 /// The `reserved` part is used for the Axiom::tag and the Axiom::sub.
40 /// Each `Char6x` is 6-bit wide and hence a dialect name has at most Axiom::Max_Dialect_Size = 8 chars.
41 /// It uses this encoding:
42 /// | `Char6` | ASCII |
43 /// |---------|---------|
44 /// | 1: | `_` |
45 /// | 2-27: | `a`-`z` |
