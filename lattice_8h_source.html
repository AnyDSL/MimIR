27/// Specific [Bound](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Join_and_meet) depending on @p up.

30/// * @p up = `true`: [Join](@ref thorin::Join) (aka least upper bound/supremum/union)

114/// `test value, probe, match, clash` tests whether [value](@ref Test::value) currently holds **type** [probe](@ref Test::probe).
