Detailed Description

Transitively visits all reachable Scopes in World that do not have free variables.

We call these Scopes top-level Scopes. Select with elide_empty whether you want to visit trivial Scopes of noms without body. Assumes that you don't change anything - hence dirty flag is set to false.
